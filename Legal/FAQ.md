# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I get an invite?

Naar's Cars is an open community. Anyone may download the app and submit an application to join the community with the intention of helping neighbors with rides and favors and in return have a community of neighbors to help you.

## How long does account approval take?

Most accounts are reviewed for safety and approved within 24 hours.

## How do I request a ride or favor?

From the home screen, tap the + button to create a new ride or favor request. Add details like the date, time, pickup/dropoff location, and any notes. Your request will be visible to all community members.

## How do I claim a request?

Browse available requests on the dashboard or map view. When you find one you can help with, tap Claim to let the requester know you're available. You'll be connected via messaging to coordinate details.

## How do I send a message?

Open any conversation from the Messages tab. You can send text, photos (from camera or library), voice notes, and your current location using the + button in the message input bar.

## How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap and hold any message, post, or comment to see the report flag option. Select a reason and submit. Community admins review all reports and take action as needed.

## How do I block another user?

When reporting a message, you'll see an option to block the sender. Blocked users cannot message you. Additionally when viewing a user's public profile from requests (tapping on a user's avatar) you can block a user from the menu on the top right of the user's profile page.

## How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → scroll to Delete Account. This permanently deletes your account and unlinks it with all associated data including rides, favors, messages, reviews, and posts. This action cannot be undone.

## How do I change my notification preferences?

Go to Profile → Settings → Notifications to control which types of notifications you receive.

## How do I enable or disable biometric unlock?

Go to Profile → Settings and toggle Face ID / Touch ID on or off.

## Is my data shared or sold?

No. Naar's Cars does not sell your data, serve ads, or track you across apps. We collect only what's needed to make the app work. See our Privacy Policy for details.
